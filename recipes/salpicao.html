Ingredients

  • 500g of shredded chicken
  • 2 shredded carrots
  • 1 can of canned peas
  • 1 can of canned corn
  • 200g of raisings

  • Mayo to taste
  • Green onion to taste
  • Batata palha to taste

Steps

  1. Mix everything minus the batata palha together.

  3. It's ready to be served.
